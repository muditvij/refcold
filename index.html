Our Smart Workflow: From Farm to Profit

  1. Load & Track: Farmer loads produce into the DEI-coldStore. AI sensors begin tracking.
  2. AI Analysis: The system monitors conditions and sends real-time data to the app.
  3. Freshness Status: The app displays a GREEN status if fresh, or a RED status if not.
  4. Market Visibility: Buyers see the real-time location and freshness of available produce on the map.
  5. Direct Purchase: A buyer gets directions and purchases directly, increasing the farmer's profit margin.
